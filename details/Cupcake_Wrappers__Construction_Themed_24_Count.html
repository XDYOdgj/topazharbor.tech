Construction Themed Cupcake Wrappers

These chic and stylish cupcake wrappers bring a bold, construction-themed twist to any party or event. Featuring eye-catching designs like yellow and orange road signs, dump trucks, and rugged steel textures, they add character and personality to desserts and small treats. Whether it's a builder's birthday party, an 'under construction' baby shower, or a ribbon-cutting ceremony, these wraps are versatile and perfect for creating a memorable atmosphere.

Designed for ease of use, these bottomless wraps are ideal for a variety of occasions. They fit standard-sized cupcakes and can also be used as drink sleeves, appetizer holders, or table accents. Made with heavy-weight, recyclable paper in the USA, they offer a durable and professional finish while being eco-friendly. Their reversible design lets you use them on both sides, providing even more options for creative decoration.

With a secure slide-in tab and slot system, these cupcake decorating tools are easy to assemble and perfect for on-the-go events. Their unique design makes them a standout addition to any celebration, while the quality ensures they last through the festivities and beyond.
