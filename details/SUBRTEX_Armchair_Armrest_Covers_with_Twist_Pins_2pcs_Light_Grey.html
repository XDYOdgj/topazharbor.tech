Elevate Your Space with SUBRTEX Stretch Armrest Covers

Give your furniture a new lease of life with our stylish and practical armrest covers. Designed to fit most fabric chairs, sofas, and recliners, they deliver comfort and protection without the need for complete furniture replacement.

Premium Comfort and Durability

SubrTex Stretch Armrest Covers are made from a high-quality blend of 95% polyester and 5% spandex, offering unmatched softness and flexibility. This material ensures a snug fit that moves with your furniture, making them both comfortable and long-lasting.

Easy Installation with Secure Fit

Our covers feature 10 invisible twist pins that provide a secure, slip-free fit. Whether you're updating your living room or adding an extra touch to your office, they're easy to install and remove, and machine washable for effortless cleaning.

Versatile Design for All Settings

Perfect for homes with kids or pets, SubrTex covers help keep your furniture looking clean and new. They're ideal for both residential and commercial use, offering an affordable solution that enhances the appearance and durability of your upholstery.

Style That Lasts

Whether you have a loveseat, standard armchair, or plush recliner, these stretch armrest covers adapt effortlessly to most fabric armrest sizes. They're perfect for those who want to refresh their decor without compromising on quality or ease of use.
